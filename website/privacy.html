Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Stilla is a premium meditation and breathwork app. This policy explains what data Stilla uses, what stays on your device, and what is processed in the cloud.

On-Device Data

Most of your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your device.

  • Breathing and sound preferences
  • Meditation history and milestones
  • Saved Kai sessions and library tags

Kai AI Processing

When you use Kai, your prompt and duration are sent to Stilla's cloud service to generate your personalized meditation. We use industry-standard language models to craft these experiences.

Permissions

  • Speech Recognition: Only used when you speak to Kai.
  • HealthKit: Optional integration to write Mindful Minutes.
  • Siri: Used for hands-free voice shortcuts.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at support@stilla.app.
