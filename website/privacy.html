Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Vindla is a premium meditation and breathwork app. This policy explains what data Vindla uses, what stays on your device, and what is processed in the cloud.

On-Device Data

Most of your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your device.

  • Breathing and sound preferences
  • Meditation history and milestones
  • Saved Mimir sessions and library tags

Mimir AI Processing & Sharing

When you use Mimir, your mood and duration are sent anonymously through Vindla's secure servers to OpenAI's commercial API to craft your personalized meditation. Your prompts and usage data are never used to train OpenAI's models.

If you choose to create a Shareable Link of a Mimir session, the anonymized script is temporarily stored on our servers for up to 30 days to allow others to view it. This shared data contains no personally identifiable information.

Permissions & iCloud

  • Speech Recognition: Only used when you speak to Mimir.
  • HealthKit: Optional integration to write Mindful Minutes.
  • Siri: Used for hands-free voice shortcuts.
  • iCloud: Used securely via your Apple ID to sync your meditation preferences and history across your personal devices.
