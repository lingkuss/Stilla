Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Vindla is a premium meditation and breathwork app. This policy explains what data Vindla uses, what stays on your device, and what is processed in the cloud.

On-Device Data

Most of your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your device.

  • Breathing and sound preferences
  • Meditation history and milestones
  • Saved Mimir sessions and library tags

Mimir AI Processing

When you use Mimir, your prompt and duration are sent to Vindla's cloud service to generate your personalized meditation. We use industry-standard language models to craft these experiences.

Permissions

  • Speech Recognition: Only used when you speak to Mimir.
  • HealthKit: Optional integration to write Mindful Minutes.
  • Siri: Used for hands-free voice shortcuts.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at support@vindla.app.
